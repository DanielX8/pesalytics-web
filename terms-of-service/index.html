2. Description of Service

Pesalytics is an offline-first Android application developed by Daniel Odhiambo. The app reads M-PESA SMS messages locally on your device and parses them into a personal finance dashboard — categorised transactions, spending totals, and trends. There is no server-side processing: Pesalytics does not send your SMS content or financial data to any backend we operate.
